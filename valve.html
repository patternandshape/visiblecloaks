VALVE

Valve is an interactive digital environment by Brenna Murphy with music by Visible Cloaks, utilizing MIDI data created out the digital translation of the voice of Miyako Koda. To start, open the application - use the arrow keys (←↑↓→) to move and the mouse to look around. Press CMD Q (⌘Q) to quit. PC version coming soon!
