VALVE

VALVE is an interactive digital environment by Brenna Murphy with music by Visible Cloaks, utilizing MIDI data created out the digital translation of the voice of Miyako Koda. To enter the environment, choose the appropriate operating system and open the downloaded application - use the arrow keys to move and the mouse to look around.
